Decision Donut

Decision Donut, was added in Poxi Games 2, unlike all other games you cannot get points from it, nor do you get indiv stats. It is the only game that is played several times.

How it works: The Decision Donut, also known as the "Donut" is played before all but the last minigame, it allows everyteam to having a saying in what minigame they would like to play.

You spawn in the center of the Donut and around you split like a pie chart are 8 diffrent floor-colored-blocks, you get a wooden sword with 15 durability, and a bonus item. There are 15 total bonus items that all have a rarity level.
You can vote by standing on the colored concrete that represents the minigame you want. Living pigs also count as votes so keep that in mind. If you ever die in the "Donut" you will not be able to vote.
