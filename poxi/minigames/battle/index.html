Battle

Battle, was added in Poxi Games 2, it's also the main PVP game of the event.

How it works: Battle has 2 rounds with 2 diffrent kits, a sword kit and axe kit. Each kit also gives you a certain amount of health (40 -> 50). There are 2 ways of getting points in Battle:

- Kills (giving 100 points)

- Damage (giving ~1 point per half heart)

- Bowing (giving ~11.2 point shot)
