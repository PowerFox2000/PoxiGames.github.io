Hunt

Hunt, was added in Poxi Games 2, and is the game with the longest developpment time (about 2-3 months)

How it works: There are 4 rounds with each round a diffrent team as hunters (Red->Green->Blue->Purple). Hunt is basically Hide & Seek, the game will select a 7 random rooms out of a pool of 15, and will place them around the center room (which is what causes the lag spike), each round will randomize the rooms. Each round lasts 1:50 seconds.

As a seeker, you are first placed in a "waiting" cage for 20 seconds to let hiders time to hide, and then you are realseased. You get 100 points per kill. You are also given a bow and a iron sword. Though your strongest weapon is the SONAR, which is used everytime you shift, when used it will tell you how many players are in a 15 block radius of you, when you detect someone, they will be warned. SONAR reloads in 3-6 seconds. You also get speed 2, jumpboost 3 and glowing.

As a Hider, you get 2 points per second alive. And if you manage to surive the round, you get 30 points.
