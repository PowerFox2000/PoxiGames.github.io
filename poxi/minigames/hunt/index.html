Hunt

Hunt, was added in Poxi Games 2, and is the game with the longest developpment time (about 2-3 months) How it works: There are 4 rounds with each round a diffrent team as hunters (Red->Green->Blue->Purple). Hunt is basically Hide & Seek, the game will select a 7 random rooms out of a pool of 15, and will place them around the center room (which is what causes the lag spike)
