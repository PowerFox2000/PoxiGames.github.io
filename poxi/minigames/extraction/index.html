Extraction

Extraction, was added in Poxi Games 3, it's currently the 2nd highest ranked minigame after Pirates.
How it works: Each team plays agaisnt another team 2 times, meaning that there's 6 rounds, most rounds last under 2 minutes though.
As a player you have 5 lives (indicated by your XP bar) and a quick-charge 3.
You're object is simple, wipe out the enemy team and then extract. You can extract by continusly holding shitf in the middle capture point.
When you shoot someone, you will get 2 visual confirmations, one on your screen telling you who you shot and their health, the other is in chat. You gain 20 points per hit.
Once you manage to kill someone, you'll gain 15 points, as well as they will release a firework. Once all the players either extracted or got killed, if you're alive you'll get additional 10 points.
