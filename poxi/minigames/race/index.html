Race

Race, was added in Poxi Games 2, it's the oldest minigame of Poxi Games 2, Race is the minigame with the most amount of maps ever used: OG, Dimensions and currently: Intersections.



How it works: You spawn at the starting line, and your objective is to complete 5 laps, currently 11 Checkpoints per lap.
Every Checkpoint gives "Theoretical Points" and those decreese every second, the "Theoretical Points" gets capped at 3, and you receive them as points once you reach the next Checkpoint.
Laps points are exaclty the same but is capped at 50, and starts with a much higher number, those points are shown on your info bar at all times.
