Heist

Heist, was added in Poxi Games 2, it's possibly one of the most complex minigames of Poxi Games. It received an update in Poxi Games 3, it's currently the biggest game of Poxi Games. And is also tied with Pirates for longest game: 20min



How it works: You spawn in the robbery van, infront of it is the bank, the bank has 3 floors, the higher the floor the more higher the rewards.
There are 4 mineable blocks in the bank, they each have diffrent value and weight: Iron [1;2], Gold [4,2], Diamond [10,3], Netherite [3,0].
They only valuable that you can't deposite is the Netherite and it will remain in your inventory. One of the most important things of Heist is to never under any circumstance punch a player, or worse kill any. You will face massive consequances if you do.
On the left side of the map you can find the warehouse, home to the upgrades. There you can get 5 different upgrades.
There are 4 ways of getting points, the main one being depositing valuables to the van; you can also get points from killing mobs, 5 per mob; you will find sculk shreikers in the warehouse, mining them will also get you 10 points. Finally you get points when getting an upgrade, depends on the tier you get.
