Bridge

Bridge, was added in Poxi Games 2, it's currently the 3nd highest ranked minigame after Pirates and Extraction. It's also the last game you can play and the only game that is a 2v2 with the other 2 teams having to watch.

How it works: Bridge takes the 2 teams with the most amount of points, and makes them 1v1, first to 5 points wins the entire event.
To get a point, you need a player from your team to jump in the oposite team's hole.

Bridge is also the only game to have no countdown and to start instantly, it's also the only game to use diffrent colors than the normal 4.
The color will always be the same and only depends on which are the 2 teams that are playing: The orange team will always be the one with the least amount of letters to spell the name or in other words, the first in priority.
