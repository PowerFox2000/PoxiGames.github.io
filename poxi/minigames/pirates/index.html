Pirates

Pirates, was the 6th Minigame coded for Poxi Games 2. It's currently rated as the best Poxi Games Minigame.

How it works: Each team is attributed a ship with 7 spawns. You are given 2 stacks of blocks, a stone sword, 5 golden apples and 10 golden carrots, as well as 32 arrows, your pickaxe and shears. With those you are bound to cross the oceans by making bridges, currently you get 120points per spawn breaking and 60 points per kills.
