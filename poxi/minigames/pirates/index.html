Pirates

Pirates, was the 6th Minigame coded for Poxi Games 2. It's currently rated as the best Poxi Games Minigame.
How it works: Each team is attributed a ship with 7 spawns. You are given 2 stacks of blocks, a stone sword, 5 golden apples and 10 golden carrots, as well as 32 arrows, your pickaxe and shears. With those you are bound to cross the oceans by making bridges, currently you get 120 points per spawn breaking and 60 points per kills.
Once you mined a spawn, it alerts everyone, and your pickaxe disapears, you will receive a new one after 15 seconds, you will be warned 5 seconds before receveing one. When you break a spawn, the block get's replaced with a spruce log. After killing 2 players without dying, you will receive 2 golden carrots, 6 arrows and an additional stack of blocks
If your team has no more spawns left, you're respawn time will automatically go from 5 seconds to 30.
The game will automatically end once either the 20 minutes passed or once all the spawns from 3 ships are destroyed, if the last condition is the case, the team that still has spawns will gain a certain amount of points depending on the amount of spawns they have (15; 25; 30; 35; 38; 40; 42). Logs:
